## General

### **What is MemoGarden?**

### **Is MemoGarden free to use?**

## Boards & Widgets

### **What can I put on my board?**
You can add notes, images, weather widgets, plant reminders, shopping lists, and more. The widget system is extensible, so new types will be added regularly.

### **Can I resize, rotate, or move widgets?**
Yes! Most widgets can be freely dragged, resized, rotated, and layered anywhere on your board.

### **Are boards private or public?**
You can create both private and public boards. Public boards can be discovered in the ‚ÄúGarden‚Äù and shared with others.

### **Can I collaborate with others on a board?**
Yes! Invite others to your board for real-time collaboration. You‚Äôll see their cursors and changes instantly.

## Technical

### **What tech stack does MemoGarden use?**
- React, Vite, TypeScript, shadcn/ui, Tailwind CSS (frontend)
- Supabase (backend, auth, real-time, storage)
- @dnd-kit (drag-and-drop), react-rnd (resize/rotate)

### **Can I self-host MemoGarden?**
Not yet, but self-hosting instructions will be provided after the first public release.

### **Is there an API?**
Yes! See [api.md](./api.md) for details on using the REST API.
